=> Steps to prepare daily DCFC charging report.


* To download the data...

	- Go to "novo.matter.in" and login.
	- Under Your Organizations Click "Matter Energy".
	- Click "Continue to Dashboard". 
	- Report & Analytics > Select Report Type "Chargers".
	- Select Report > "Sessions".
	- Filter by Hub > Select all Hub and Apply.
	- Select Date range for charging report.
	- Note: Click "Table field" and check or select the "Selected fields" as per this Sequence "Hub Name", "Device ID", "Session ID", "Driver Details", "Usage", 
 		"Start Time", "End Time", "Duration" , "Status", "Start SoC", "End SoC", "Vin Number". after sequence is ok then Click apply.
	- Click "Generate Report".
	

* To the generate report....

	- Open chrome browser then copy below link and Paste the below link in the address bar:

       	https://fc-daily-charging-report-7nsjtuqy2sfk7fsvpp8cbd.streamlit.app/

	- Click Browse File.
	- Upload the downloaded Session Report Excel file.
	- Click "Generate Final PDF".
	- After processing, click Download PDF Report.

Note: Check Dashboard sheet for verifying the charts area covering all data properly, if not proper then make chart size proper and change print area accordingly.
